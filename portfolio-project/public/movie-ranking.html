The Best Movies According to Tania

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Weak Hero Class 1 & 2

This is my absolute favourite because of the intense action and the way the characters stand up for themselves against all odds. It's gripping from start to finish

Bloodhounds

I love this because of the incredible bond between the main characters. Their loyalty to each other while navigating such a dangerous world is so inspiring to watch.

Happy Gilmore

This is my go-to Adam Sandler movie. I love it because it is hilarious, and his character's unique approach to golf always manages to put me in a great mood whenever I need a laugh.
